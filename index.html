Natural Systems Laboratories builds AI/ML based solutions to better understand the physical world around us. We focus on geophysical problems such as acoustic detection of friction and fracturing, fluid flows, and other subsurface phenomena. We also develop time-series predictors for event prediction.

We receive funding from a variety of sources including the Norwegian Research Council and viewers like you. Contact us: info@nslaboratories.com
